How to create an account

To create a Sonix Movies account:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the homepage.
  2. Enter your email address and choose a strong password.
  3. Fill in your profile information (name, date of birth, etc.).
  4. Agree to the terms of service and privacy policy.
  5. Click "Create Account" to finish the process.
